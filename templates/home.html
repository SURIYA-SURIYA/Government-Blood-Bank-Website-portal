தமிழ்நாடு அரசு இரத்த வங்கிக்கு வரவேற்கிறோம் | இரத்த வலை போர்டல் தேவைப்பட்டால் இரத்த தானம் செய்வதையோ அல்லது இரத்தத்தைப் பெறுவதையோ பரிசீலிக்கவும்.

A blood bank is a vital facility that ensures a steady and safe supply of blood for patients in need. Blood banks collect, test, store, and distribute blood and its components, such as plasma and platelets, for transfusions. Donated blood helps save lives in emergencies, during surgeries, and for individuals with medical conditions like cancer, anemia, or severe trauma. Maintaining an adequate blood supply is crucial to healthcare systems, and blood banks rely heavily on regular, voluntary donations from healthy individuals. By donating blood, you can contribute directly to saving lives and supporting the health of your community.

Our Vision

At the Government Blood Bank, our vision is to create a world where every individual has access to safe and sufficient blood when needed. We aspire to be a leader in the field of blood donation and transfusion services, setting the standard for excellence and compassion in healthcare. We aim to cultivate a strong culture of voluntary blood donation within our communities. By inspiring individuals to contribute to the health and well-being of others, we hope to increase the number of regular donors and raise awareness about the importance of blood donation. Our vision is to ensure a safe, reliable, and equitable supply of blood and blood components for all citizens in need. We strive to foster a culture of voluntary blood donation, promote public awareness, and leverage advanced technology to streamline blood donation, storage, and distribution processes.
